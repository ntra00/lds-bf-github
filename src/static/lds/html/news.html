News
National Library Catalog (beta)

February 15, 2012

Library of Congress Web Archives records added.

Library of Congress Web Archives records are now included in the National Library Catalog. New features not available in the original display system include thumbnails of the web site archived shown with the bibliographic information, and links to the crawled versions of the site are listed directly on the page, for easier access the the archives.

January 31, 2012

Support for Zotero added.

Search results and detail records in the National Library Catalog are now downloadable to Zotero (via MODS).  Zotero, a Firefox browser plugin, is a research tool that allows you to save bibliographic reference information from the web to your personal library catalog.

September 6, 2011

Holdings information has been added to individual record displays.

Library of Congress holdings information has been added to the National Library Catalog (beta). Displayed at the bottom of each individual record, this information includes the library location where materials can be requested as well as call number(s)/shelving locations for each copy or volume of a work. Another benefit gained by adding holdings to the catalog is that we can now provide more accurate "facets" for "Library Location." Facets, used to further refine search results, are found in the left column of the results screen.

Holdings information was the "most requested" new feature from our users, so we are very eager to get your feedback. A “send us your feedback” link is included on every page of the catalog. You may alternatively send comments directly to natlibcat@loc.gov.

April 22, 2011

Library Services announced the beta release of the National Library Catalog (beta).

The National Library Catalog is an online catalog that offers a new way to access the Library of Congress collections.  This initial beta release contains all the records from the Library's traditional catalog (catalog.loc.gov).  In the next few months, the National Library Catalog will grow significantly as records from a wide range of LC collections are added.  These additions will include integrated holdings records from the catalog, digital content such as books, sheet music, photos, audio, video, and full text content.  LC authority records will be added, enabling enhanced searching capabilities.   Programming enhancements will enrich searching by adding filters for publication place and geographic subject, and improving date searching. Citation formatting features will also be developed. 

We are eager to get feedback from staff during this initial beta release. A “send us your feedback” link is included on every page.  You may alternatively send comments directly to natlibcat@loc.gov.
